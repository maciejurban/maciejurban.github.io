Hotailors

Currently I am working on redesigning the SaaS tool for planning business trips. The main objectives are to simplify the interface and add new functions. We decided that we also will make it easier for the Hotailors team to design in the future by creating a basic design system.

We are designing based on remote observation of users, workshops with Hotailors team and internal workshops

I am project co-lead, and I am responsible for UX and UI.

Due to ongoing status I will present concepts from the beginning of the project.
